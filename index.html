OAH Deadline Generator Instructions

Required Fields

Enter the complaint served date, PHC date and start time, DPH start date, and PHC statement/motions due date before generating deadlines. A warning will appear if one of these are missing.

Optional Fields

Aside from the required above, all other fields are options. Mediation in particular should be noted. If no mediation date is entered, the mediation row and mediation participation deadline will display as blank placeholders once deadlines are generated.

Exports

The DOCX export creates a Word OAH deadline sheet. The ICS export creates an Outlook calendar file, allowing you ot quickly import the dates to your own calendar, and copy them to another. If you change any inputs, you must generate deadlines again before exporting.
